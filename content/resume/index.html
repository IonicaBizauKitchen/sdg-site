Overview

I am part designer, part developer born and raised near the shore of West Michigan currently working as the Creative Lead for Los Angeles-based company Flipswap. I have designed print collateral, produced web/magazine/video ads, developed brand guides, and planned/launched products into “the cloud” for the past 3 years. But, most of my work from the past 8 years is in the form of easy-to-use, standards-compliant, beautiful websites for Flipswap and my company, Sentinel Design Group. I am extremely passionate about the work I do.
