What is Sentinel Design?

SD is…

Sentinel Design is Drew Yeaton’s creative design company that takes a hands-on approach to the web. We love to get excited about our client’s projects—we love creating a purposeful, fulfilling experience for your clients. We do this with a confident approach to simple design. When we’re through with a project, your company's personality will show through in the finished product.

SD loves…

Sentinel Design is fortunate enough to work with organizations that are as enthusiastic and passionate about their business as we are. This is what we love about what we do.

Sentinel Design loves typography, colors and good user interfaces. SD loves your product just as much as you do and will do anything to make it (and you) look good.
