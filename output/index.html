Imagining the Charity Ecosystem

Creating a visual identity around Flipswap's charity is a challenge Sentinel Design Group took on recently.

The idea was to portray the fun nature of creating something new with Flipswap (ie. a charity partnership) while minimizing the confusion about how it all works. We landed on a solution that involves a large Naïve art mural with some direct and meaningful copy. This design, which borders on visually chaotic, turns out to be quite simple and easy to swallow.
